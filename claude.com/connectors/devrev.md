Connect Computer and Claude to search, create, and action work across your product and customer operations.

Bring your workspace into Claude and take action on what matters.

Whether you're a developer triaging issues from the terminal, a support lead reviewing tickets, or a product manager planning the next sprint, the Computer connector lets you work in a seamless environment.

Search your knowledge graph, manage issues and tickets, update contacts and accounts, and link work across your entire organisation through a simple conversation with Claude.

You can use Computer in Claude to:

**Search:**"Find all enhancements related to the onboarding flow"

**Triage and manage:**"Show me all open P1 tickets assigned to my team"

**Create:**"Create a bug report for the login timeout issue and assign it to the platform team"

**Stay connected:**"Link this conversation to ticket don:core:dvrv-us-1:devo/0:ticket/204"

**Update:**"Update the status of issue don:core:dvrv-us-1:devo/0:issue/88 to in progress"
