Search, summarize, create, and update projects, tasks, workspaces, planning records and approvals directly from Claude. Make Adobe Workfront a native part of your AI workflow — an AI-powered work management agent that helps you stay on top of deadlines, surface risks, and move work forward faster.

You can use Adobe Workfront to:

**Status in seconds:**  
"Summarize the status of every project in the 'Spring Campaign' portfolio and flag anything at risk."

**Create work:**  
"Create a new Workfront task under project 'Website Redesign' called 'Finalize hero copy', assign it to me, due Friday."

**Clear approvals:**  
"List all approvals waiting on me and approve the ones from the brand team."
