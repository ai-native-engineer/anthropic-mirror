Access Google Meet, Zoom & Teams meeting transcripts. Yours, and those from your team & company.

You can use tldv to:

**Pull a transcript:**  
"Get the transcript from yesterday's product sync."

**Summarize a meeting:**  
"What were the action items from the Acme kickoff call?"

**Search across meetings:**  
"Find every meeting this month where we discussed the pricing change."

**Catch up:**  
"Give me the notes from the three calls I missed while I was out."
