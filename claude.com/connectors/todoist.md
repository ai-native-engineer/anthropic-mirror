Allow Claude to assist you in managing your productivity and time blocking, by searching your current tasks, completing them, and general management of tasks in Todoist.

You can use Todoist to:

**See today's tasks:**  
"Summarise my tasks for today"

**Reschedule overdue tasks:**  
"I have some tasks that are currently overdue, can you please reschedule them for tomorrow"

**Create new tasks:**  
"I need you to look into <topic> and break down the results into small actionable tasks"

**Summarize completed work:**  
"Can you summarize all the completed tasks in my Work project that I completed last week"

**Add comments to tasks:**  
"Looking at the following task, can you research whether the issue is valid, and if it isn't, add a comment explaining what has been found"
