Manage your partner program and access your Partner Data Lake with Claude — instant actions and answers on deals, commissions, onboarding, and partner performance, no dashboard required. Partners can register deals and access live data across their pipeline, enablement, certifications, payouts and more.

You can use EULER to:

**Check partner performance:**  
"How is Acme tracking against their Q2 commission tier?"

**Look up deals and invoices:**  
"Show me open deals from partners in the EMEA region this quarter."

**Submit a referral:**  
"Log a referral for Contoso from partner Northwind with a target close of June 30."

**Manage onboarding flows:**  
"What step is the new partner Globex on in the onboarding flow, and who is it assigned to?"
