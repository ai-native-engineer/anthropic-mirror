Era Context is a secure place to manage your finances. Set up automations, fix overspending, figure out your portfolio, or tell Claude a goal and get your full financial life in context.

You can use Era Context to:

**Spending Automation:**  
"Analyze my finances and suggest rules to automatically categorize and tidy up my transactions."

**Credit Card Optimization:**  
"Analyze my credit card spending and tell me how to maximize my rewards points based on where I spend the most."

**Subscription Audit:**  
"Am I overpaying for any of my subscriptions? Check if I have duplicate charges or if cheaper alternatives exist."

**Financial Goal Setting:**  
"I want to save $10,000 by the end of the year — based on my income and spending habits, tell me if that's realistic and what I need to change."

**Smart Spending:**  
"Based on how much I spend on gas, which fuel rewards program would save me the most money?"
