The TomTom Maps MCP Server simplifies geospatial development by providing seamless access to TomTom's location services, including search, routing, traffic, and dynamic map rendering with native modules bundled.

You can use TomTom Maps to:

**Geocode addresses:**  
"Find the coordinates for 1600 Pennsylvania Avenue and show nearby points of interest"

**Plan a route:**  
"Calculate the fastest driving route from Amsterdam to Berlin with current traffic"

**Find EV charging:**  
"Locate EV charging stations within 5km of my current location"

**Check traffic:**  
"What's the current traffic situation on the A10 ring road?"
