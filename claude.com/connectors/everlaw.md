Combine Everlaw's powerful AI and analytics with your Claude-based workflows. Connect Everlaw to Claude to search, analyze, and pull case insights in seconds—right from your documents. Stay in control and move faster on live matters without risking confidentiality or defensibility.

You can use Everlaw to:

**Search and summarize a large document in your project:**  
"In Project ID 2, retrieve and summarize the document with Bates Number #509574."

**Visualize and QA a large set of documents in your project:**  
"In Project ID 2, visualize the volume of documents containing the word 'energy', grouped by year."

**QA and analyze your document set in your binders:**  
"In Project ID 2, analyze the metadata in my binders and flag any data gaps."
