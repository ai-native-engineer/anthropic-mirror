You can use Vani by Zoho to:

**Spin up a workspace:**  
"Create a new Vani Space called 'Q3 Launch Plan' with sections for goals, timeline, and risks."

**Summarize a discussion:**  
"Summarize the discussion in my 'Roadmap Review' Space and list the decisions we landed on."

**Build a visual:**  
"Draft a mind map in Vani of the feature ideas we brainstormed in this conversation."
