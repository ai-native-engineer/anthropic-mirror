Create beautifully structured documents, manage tasks, and build a personal knowledge base - all directly from Claude. Give Claude a persistent workspace to save research, draft content, track to-dos, and organize everything into folders, so your best thinking lives beyond the conversation.

You can use Craft to:

**Search & Summarize Your Notes:**  
"Find my notes about [topic] and summarize the key points"

**Create & Organize Documents:**  
"Create a new document in [folder] with sections for [what you need]"

**Manage Tasks & Review Your Week:**  
"Add a task to tomorrow's daily note" or "Summarize what I got done this week from my daily notes"

**Build Personal Databases:**  
"Create a collection with columns for [fields] and add these entries"
