Otto brings business travel bookings into Claude. Search flights and hotels, compare options, then book your choice. Your preferences filter search results (cabin class, seat, hotel brand, airline), and your loyalty numbers attach to reservations. When plans change, exchange or cancel a flight or hotel. It retrieves trip details on request: flight times, hotel addresses, confirmation numbers.

You can use Otto Travel to:

**Book a business trip:**  
"Find me a nonstop flight from SFO to JFK next Tuesday morning and a hotel within walking distance of our Midtown office, then book both within policy."

**Compare options:**  
"Show me three flight options from London to Berlin on June 20th sorted by departure time, with my preferred airline first."

**Manage an itinerary:**  
"Pull up my trip to Chicago next week and add a rental car pickup at O'Hare."
