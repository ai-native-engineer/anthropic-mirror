Run active and on-demand Make scenarios, view and modify Make scenarios and their related entities (e.g., connections, webhooks, and data stores), and view and modify teams and organizations - all from within Claude.

You can use Make to:

**Run Scenarios:**  
"Execute my customer onboarding automation"

**Manage Workflows:**  
"Show me all active scenarios in my account"

**Configure Connections:**  
"Update the Slack connection in my lead processing scenario"

**Monitor Data:**  
"Check the status of my CRM synchronization workflow"
