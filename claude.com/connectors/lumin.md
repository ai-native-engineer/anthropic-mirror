Connect Claude to Lumin PDF's document management services. Manage documents, send signature requests, retrieve workspace details, and convert Markdown to PDF through natural-language commands.

You can use Lumin to:

**Document Management:**  
"Upload this contract to my Lumin workspace and organize it in the legal documents folder."

**Signature Requests:**  
"Send this NDA to [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection) for digital signature and notify me when it's completed."

**Workspace Organization:**  
"Show me all documents in my shared workspace and their current status."

**PDF Creation:**  
"Convert this markdown document into a professional PDF and upload it to my workspace."
