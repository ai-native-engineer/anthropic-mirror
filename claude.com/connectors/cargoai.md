Browse rates and track air cargo directly from Claude. Make CargoAi a native part of your AI workflow — an AI-powered freight agent that helps forwarders get instant quotes and monitor shipments faster, with less friction. Bring your CargoMart operations into the conversation.

You can use CargoAi to:

**Get instant rates:**  
"Get me air cargo rates for 500kg of general cargo from FRA to JFK departing next week."

**Track a shipment:**  
"Track AWB 020-12345678 and tell me its current location and ETA."

**Compare carriers:**  
"Compare quotes from three carriers for 2 pallets HKG to LAX and recommend the best balance of price and transit time."
