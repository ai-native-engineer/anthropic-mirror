Browse your Listen Labs studies, pull response data, review study designs, and surface analysis insights directly from Claude. Make Listen Labs a native part of your research workflow, an AI-powered research assistant that helps you synthesize qualitative findings faster, with deeper insight.

You can use Listen Labs to:

**Browse & Discover Studies:**  
"Show me all my active studies"

**Review Study Design:**  
"Pull up the discussion guide for my pricing research study"

**Analyze Responses:**  
"Summarize the key themes from respondents in my brand perception study"

**Deep-Dive on Individual Responses:**  
"What did respondent #3 say about onboarding friction?"

**Cross-Response Synthesis:**  
"Compare how power users vs. new users described their checkout experience"

**Surface Actionable Insights:**  
"What are the top unmet needs across all responses in my feature discovery study?"
