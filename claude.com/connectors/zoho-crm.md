You can use Zoho CRM to:

**Manage leads and opportunities:**  
"Create a new lead for John Carter from Acme Inc with email [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection)"

**Track deals and revenue:**  
"Find all open deals worth more than $25,000 and show closing dates"

**Update pipeline status:**  
"Update the deal Q1 Enterprise Upgrade to stage Negotiation and add follow-up task"

**Organize customer data:**  
"Get all contacts from the Technology industry and their recent activities"
