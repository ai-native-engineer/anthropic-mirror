Insider MCP enables AI-powered clients to securely query Insider One CDP and APIs using natural language. It provides controlled, read-only access to Insider CDP and APIs, while fully respecting existing authentication, permission, and governance models.

Designed for enterprise use, Insider MCP allows teams to explore data, retrieve insights, and query platform objects without navigating dashboards or writing custom queries. All requests are permission-based, transparent, and executed through predefined APIs, ensuring security, control, and scalability as capabilities expand over time.

You can use Insider One to:

**List campaigns:**  
"Show me my Insider One email campaigns from the last 30 days"

**Analyze a campaign:**  
"Get the analytics for my Black Friday email campaign"

**See the big picture:**  
"Summarize my overall email performance this quarter"
