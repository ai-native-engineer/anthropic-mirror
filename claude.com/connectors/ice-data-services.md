Query real-time and historical bond trades across corporate, agency and securitized products reported to FINRA TRACE, and municipal bond trades reported to MSRB RTRS. Look up bond reference data including coupon details and maturity dates.

You can use ICE Data Services to:

**Monitor Institutional Trading Activity:**  
"Show me the 10 largest corporate bond trades today"

**Analyze Sector Trading Volume:**  
"Compare trading volume across financials, industrials, and utilities this week"

**Research an Issuer's Bond Portfolio:**  
"Find all Apple bonds and tell me about their maturity profile"

**Classify Financial Concepts:**  
"What is an institutional trade? Is a $500K trade considered an institutional trade?"

**Municipal Bond Analysis:**  
"Show me revenue bond trading activity for the last 5 days grouped by day"

**Sub-Sector Drill-Down with Data Provenance:**  
"Break down corporate bond volume by sector category today"

**Precise Arithmetic on Query Results:**  
"Sum the total notional across all sectors from today's corporate bond volume"
