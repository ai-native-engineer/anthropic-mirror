iManage Work Connector for MCP securely connects governed iManage content to Claude. Replace custom integrations with one open protocol, keep documents in iManage without bulk exports or replication, and ensure permission-bound, auditable Claude access as your AI strategy evolves.

You can use iManage Work to:

**Precedent Document Discovery:**  
"I need to draft a lease agreement for a property in London. Can you search iManage for similar leases we've done in the past 2 years, preferably in London?"

**Client Matter Research:**  
"I'm taking over the Acme Corp litigation matter. Can you find all documents related to this client and give me a summary of the key filings and correspondence from the last 6 months?"

**Expert Work Product Retrieval:**  
"Sarah Johnson is our IP litigation expert. Can you find all motion to dismiss briefs she's authored in patent cases? I need to see her approach to claim construction arguments."

**Due Diligence Document Review:**  
"We're doing due diligence on a pharmaceutical company. Can you find all FDA correspondence and regulatory submissions from our past pharma deals, particularly around drug approval processes?"
