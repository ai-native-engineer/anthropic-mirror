Bring context from Circleback into Claude. Search and access meeting notes, transcripts, calendar events, emails, people, companies, and more.

You can use the Circleback connector to:

Find and reference past conversations:  
"When did we last meet with someone at Initech?"

Get answers using context from your meetings:  
"Which customers requested export to PDF?"

Prepare for meetings:  
"Help me prepare for my next meeting with Ali"

Get up to speed on a relationship:  
"Summarize my recent emails and meetings with Initech"

Search calendar events:  
"What meetings do I have scheduled with the sales team this week?"

Find people and companies:  
"Show me details about contacts from Acme Corp"
