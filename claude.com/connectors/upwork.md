Upwork makes it easy to turn a simple conversation into real work, right from Claude. Describe what you need and instantly get matched with expert talent from Upwork or generate a job post in seconds. Then, move seamlessly to Upwork to hire quickly and get started.

You can use Upwork to:

**Search for freelancers:**  
"I'm looking for a freelance web developer"

**Draft jobs to Upwork:**  
"Help me draft a job post for an AI engineering project"

**Hire freelancers on Upwork:**  
"I want to hire a freelancer to build my website"
