Brings together Adobe Creative Cloud tools for images, vectors, design, and video. Edit multiple assets at once, adapt for different platforms, and complete multi-step creative workflows for polished results.

You can use Adobe for Creativity to:

* **Polished headshots.** Try asking: "Use portrait refinement to edit these headshots."
* **On-brand content.** Try asking: "Make an Instagram story for my boutique sale from the design library​."
* **Resize videos.** Try asking: "Resize this video for YouTube shorts."
* **Create social variations.** Try Asking: “Create social variations of this post for Instagram, LinkedIn, and TikTok.”
* **Batch edit your photos.** Try Asking: “Batch edit these photos — make them warm, consistent, and cinematic.”
* **Quick Cut your videos.** Try Asking: “Create a 30-second highlight reel from this video.”
