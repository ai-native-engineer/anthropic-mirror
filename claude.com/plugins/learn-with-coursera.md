Answer 3 questions for personalized Coursera recommendations—courses, projects, videos, or more. Requires connector.

Turn any learning goal into a personalized Coursera experience. This plugin acts as an AI learning advisor: it asks three quick diagnostic questions to understand your topic, current skill level, and preferred way to learn, then searches Coursera's catalog and delivers the right resource — a structured course, hands-on project, short video, or live roleplay session. After delivering content, it maps a personalized learning path forward based on your professional context and goals.

The plugin works in three steps. First, it diagnoses your needs by asking about your topic and how it applies to your role, your familiarity level (from "brand new" to "solid, want more"), and your preferred learning format. Then it searches and delivers matching content from Coursera's catalog in real time. Finally, a Path Finder step reviews course curricula, validates fit for your role, and recommends a concrete next step — with ratings, key topics, and a reason the course fits your specific situation.

**How to use:** Express any learning intent naturally — try prompts like "Teach me SQL for product management," "I want to get better at negotiation," "Help me prepare for a cloud certification," or "I need a real foundation in Python — I keep using AI but don't understand what's happening." The plugin activates automatically when it detects a learning goal. Requires the Coursera connector to be enabled for catalog search and content delivery.
