Marketing covers content creation, campaign planning, brand voice management, competitive analysis, and performance reporting. Draft blog posts, social media, email newsletters, landing pages, press releases, and case studies with `/draft-content`.

Plan full campaigns with `/campaign-plan` — get objectives, channel strategy, content calendars, and success metrics. Review any content against your brand voice and style guide with `/brand-review`, and generate competitive positioning briefs with `/competitive-brief`.

Build marketing performance reports with `/performance-report`, run comprehensive SEO audits with `/seo-audit`, and design multi-email nurture sequences with `/email-sequence`.

Configure your brand voice and style guide in local settings for automatic brand standards enforcement. Connect tools like Slack, Canva, Figma, HubSpot, Amplitude, Ahrefs, and Klaviyo via MCP for a richer workflow.
