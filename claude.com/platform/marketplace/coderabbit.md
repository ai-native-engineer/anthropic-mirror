* Use case

CodeRabbit is the collaborative AI platform for software engineering teams that need to understand, validate, and trust AI-generated code at scale. Claude powers CodeRabbit's context engine and review harness, working directly inside your Git environment, IDE, Slack, and ticketing tools, grounded in your own codebase and team conventions.

## What you can do

* **Understand agent-generated changes.** Get semantic diffs, logic diagrams, and visual walkthroughs so your team can comprehend what AI shipped.
* **Catch critical bugs before production.** Run the most thorough, agent-agnostic AI code review on every PR, catching critical issues that others miss.
* **Build shared team context.** Persistent records of decisions, conventions, and past PR intent keep every engineer and agent aligned across sessions and codebases.
* **Bring code intelligence into your workflow.** Obtain codebase understanding in Slack, from planning, review, and ticket triaging to incident response, without switching tools.
* **Set consistent quality standards.** Custom pre-merge checks and code guidelines enforce your team's bar automatically for both human and agent-written code.
