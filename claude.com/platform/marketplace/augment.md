* Use case

Cosmos by Augment Code is an agent platform for software development. Engineering organizations use it to put expert agents across the lifecycle, from triage through authoring, testing, and review, so work runs end to end and people step in only at the decisions that matter. Claude powers the reasoning behind each expert, working on your codebase, in your tools, under your policy.

## What you can do

* **Run the whole lifecycle, not just the code.** Expert agents handle triage, authoring, testing, and review. Each owns its stage and pulls in a person only at the checkpoints that matter.
* **Catch problems before they merge.** Deep Code Review reads each pull request and posts inline comments. Risk analysis flags blast radius, security exposure, and migration risk before merge.
* **Spend less per task.** The Context Engine sends each agent only the slice of your codebase a task touches, so runs cost less without losing quality.
* **Build your own experts by describing them.** Tell an agent what you need and it builds the expert, environment and all. Publish it to a shared registry for the team to use and fork.
* **Run it inside your perimeter.** Cosmos runs in your cloud or ours, on your keys, with your own model keys. We never train on your code.
