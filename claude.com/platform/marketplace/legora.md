Use Legora aOS™, the agentic operating system for legal work, to run entire matters end-to-end across jurisdictions, languages, and every stage of legal work.

## What you can do

* **Legal research:** Search across regulatory sources and case law, and get findings organized by jurisdiction.
* **Contract review:** Identify deviations from standard terms and get a prioritized list of issues to address.
* **Due diligence:** Review transaction documents at volume and flag what needs a closer look.
* **Drafting:** Generate first drafts of agreements and memos that reflect your firm's own conventions.
