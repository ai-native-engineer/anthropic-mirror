# Replit

Build and ship full-stack applications in natural language, for builders and enterprises of all sizes.

* Use case

Replit is an AI-powered platform where anyone can build, deploy, and scale production-ready apps in plain English. Enterprises, startups, and individual builders use it to ship custom software faster, without needing deep technical expertise. Claude powers Replit's Agent, translating plain-language instructions into working, deployed applications backed by your own data and knowledge.

## What you can do

* **Build apps from a single prompt.** Describe what you want in plain English and Replit Agent builds a working app, complete with a UI, backend logic, and database, ready to share or deploy.
* **Extend with MCP and integrations.** Connect Replit to your existing technology stack through our connector ecosystem, including Databricks, Salesforce, Slack, and more, to build apps that act on real enterprise data.
* **Customize and iterate without code.** Make changes by describing what you want. Non-technical teammates can build and modify apps alongside engineers, in the same Workspace.
* **Deploy with one click.** Ship directly to a production URL without managing infrastructure. Apps run on enterprise-grade hosting with security, autoscaling, and secrets management built in.
* **Ship faster as a team.** Anyone on your team, from analysts to operations to finance, can build custom applications for their specific needs without writing a single line of code, freeing up engineering resources and enabling every team to move on their own terms.
