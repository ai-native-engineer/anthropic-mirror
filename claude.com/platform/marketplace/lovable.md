Lovable empowers anyone to build, manage, and run apps or websites.

* Use case

Lovable is a software creation platform that empowers anyone to build full-stack apps and websites through conversation. Simply describe what you want to make, and Lovable creates and iterates with you as your technical partner. Claude brings reasoning to Lovable's chat mode, where users plan projects before creating. With Lovable now connected to Claude.ai natively, you can go from idea to a deployed app — ideating, building, and shipping in one motion.

## What you can do

* **Start creating instantly:** Just describe what you want to build in plain language. Lovable handles the setup, the architecture, and the code so you can focus on your vision.
* **Build from inspiration:** See an app or website you love? Screenshot and upload it. Lovable will build something inspired by it that you can tailor to your preferences.
* **Get a production-ready backend for scale:** Scalable databases, secure login, file uploads, and live updates are automatically set up for you. Add AI features like chat, image generation, and text analysis in minutes.
* **Build internal tools:** Lovable gives everyone in the workplace a way to build internal tools, landing pages, experiments, and campaigns themselves, without waiting for technical resources or competing with existing product priorities.
