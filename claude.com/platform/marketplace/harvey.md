Harvey is the operating system for legal and professional services. It is used by global law firms and Fortune 500 enterprises for contract analysis, due diligence, compliance, and litigation. Powered by Claude, Harvey gives your team a direct path to handling complex legal workflows with purpose-built AI. Everything stays grounded in your firm's documents, precedents, and trusted legal sources.

## What you can do

* **Drafting:** Ask Harvey for a first draft of any legal document: a contract, brief, memo, or filing. It draws from your precedents and adapts to the matter, so lawyers start with something real.
* **Document analysis:** Upload contracts, filings, or case materials and ask questions across thousands of documents at once. Harvey reads across all of them, answers your questions, and shows exactly where in the documents it found support.
* **Legal research:** Search across 500+ legal sources in 60+ jurisdictions and get a clear answer with citations your team can verify.
* **Contract review:** Run any agreement against your playbook and get a redline in minutes. You see what to push on before you get to the table.
* **Workflow automation:** Set up agents to handle multi-step tasks: due diligence reports, disclosure reviews, regulatory filings. Your team handles the judgment calls; Harvey handles the rest.
