## Why finance teams choose Cowork

Built for teams that spend more time formatting than analyzing.

![Cowork for Finance icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5e57fc9d77e22cee754_Files.svg)

![Cowork for Finance icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726dea209a6f9feca25adc_Files.svg)

### Documents become structured data

Point Cowork at a folder of invoices, receipts, or vendor submissions. Get back a clean spreadsheet with every line item extracted, organized, and ready to reconcile.

![Cowork for Finance icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5d73149d8120c3064d4_Chart.svg)

![Cowork for Finance icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de40e27a9b50454f302_Chart.svg)

### Models built from your data, not from scratch

Hand Cowork revenue data and assumptions. Get back a 3-statement model or DCF in Excel with sensitivity tables, formatted and formula-linked.

![Cowork for Finance icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5d05b8a3ca9fae51656_Batch.svg)

![Cowork for Finance icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de0861f7607e8bae01b_Batch.svg)

### Reach all your tools in one place

Cowork runs in the Claude desktop app and connects to your everyday tools and local files. For web workflows, Claude in Chrome goes wherever your browser takes you.

![Tool icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5ffb0b8d74135e197e8_Tool.svg)

![Tool icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726dff1439f5401deb7cfc_Tool.svg)

### You stay in control

Cowork handles the work, but you make the calls. You can choose to review outputs before they're sent, approve actions before they're taken, and stay informed every step of the way.

## Use cases for finance teams

### Invoice and receipt cleanup

Read receipts from a folder, extract vendor, date, and amount, and organize everything into a clean, sortable spreadsheet.

![Chart icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5d73149d8120c3064d4_Chart.svg)

![Chart icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de40e27a9b50454f302_Chart.svg)

### Financial model building

Take revenue data and assumptions and build a 3-statement model or DCF in Excel with sensitivity tables and linked formulas.

![Checklist icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5dad0a42ff9b1d9abad_Checklist.svg)

![Checklist icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de43add0651f34cb63e_Checklist.svg)

### Vendor onboarding review

Cross-reference vendor submission docs against your internal checklists, flag what's missing, and draft a follow-up email.

![Files icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5e57fc9d77e22cee754_Files.svg)

![Files icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726dea209a6f9feca25adc_Files.svg)

### Due diligence summaries

Analyze a data room of documents, surface key risks, and produce an investment-committee-ready summary deck.

![Activity icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5c40c7210d194cbe0f6_Activity.svg)

Import bank transactions, calculate historical patterns and seasonality, and generate a 6-month forecast with scenarios in Excel.
