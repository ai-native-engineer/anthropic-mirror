## Why sales teams choose Cowork

Built for teams who'd rather be closing.

![Cowork for Sales icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5d73149d8120c3064d4_Chart.svg)

![Cowork for Sales icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de40e27a9b50454f302_Chart.svg)

### Do more without administrative work

Send follow-ups, log deal activity, and update your pipeline. Cowork handles the repetitive tasks so you can focus on building relationships.

![Cowork for Sales icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5d791f0e441cbf8d986_Check%20Circle.svg)

![Cowork for Sales icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de429bc4ad0f9df61dc_Check%20Circle.svg)

### Get an instant briefing

Cowork pulls context from your email, calendar, and CRM and gives you a one-pager with talking points and open items.

![Cowork for Sales icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5d05b8a3ca9fae51656_Batch.svg)

![Cowork for Sales icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de0861f7607e8bae01b_Batch.svg)

### Reach all your tools in one

Cowork runs in the Claude desktop app and connects to your email, calendar, and CRM. For web, Claude in Chrome goes wherever your workflow takes you.

![Tool icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5ffb0b8d74135e197e8_Tool.svg)

![Tool icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726dff1439f5401deb7cfc_Tool.svg)

### You stay in control

Cowork handles the work, but you make the calls. You can choose to review outputs before they're sent, approve actions before they're taken, and stay informed every step of the way.

## Use cases for sales teams

![Chat Add icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5d79614cfeddc7077ec_Chat%20Add.svg)

![Chat Add icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de497952485f6a3aa80_Chat%20Add.svg)

### Follow-ups that actually go out

After a call, Cowork processes your notes or transcript, extracts action items, and drafts a follow-up email with next steps. It logs the activity to your CRM so you're always up to date.

![Activity icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5c40c7210d194cbe0f6_Activity.svg)

![Activity icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726dde898e44c770f0fb12_Activity.svg)

### Pipeline updates without the meeting

Cowork reviews your pipeline, scores deal health, flags at-risk opportunities, and generates a prioritized action plan, ready to share with your manager or team.

![Calendar icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5c47fc9d77e22cedac7_Calendar.svg)

![Calendar icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de2334995cadd74cf54_Calendar.svg)

### Meeting prep and daily briefs in minutes

Before each call, Cowork pulls account context, researches attendees, and builds a one-pager with talking points, open items, and suggested discovery questions.

![Chart icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5d73149d8120c3064d4_Chart.svg)

![Chart icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de40e27a9b50454f302_Chart.svg)

### Forecast analysis delivered on time, always

Cowork pulls your pipeline data and runs weighted projections with best, likely, and worst scenarios. You get a forecast with gap analysis and recommended actions.

![Eye icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5e06a5153d1921f5d32_Eye.svg)

![Eye icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de865711b3d8cfd2d27_Eye.svg)

### Quick, up-to-date competitive intel

Ask about a competitor and get back a differentiation matrix, pricing comparison, and talk tracks you can use on your next call.

![Files icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5e57fc9d77e22cee754_Files.svg)

![Files icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726dea209a6f9feca25adc_Files.svg)

### Helpful deal, pricing, and demo strategies

Describe your prospect and Cowork researches their company, recommends a deal approach, drafts personalized outreach, and builds custom decks or one-pagers tailored to their use case.
