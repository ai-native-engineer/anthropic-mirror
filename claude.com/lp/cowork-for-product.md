## Why product teams choose Cowork

Built for product managers juggling too many things at once.

![Cowork for Product icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5dc8ec6ab6a678e6e47_Database.svg)

![Cowork for Product icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de6740b501274d0fe7e_Database.svg)

### Get the full picture in one ask

Ask Cowork to check Asana, email, and Slack for task updates, blockers, and dependencies. Get a snapshot of where things stand, without chasing people down.

![Cowork for Product icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5e0a60feb59aaf83fc6_Feedback.svg)

![Cowork for Product icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de83add0651f34cb6eb_Feedback.svg)

### Customer signals in one place

Feature requests and feedback come through email, Slack, and support tickets. Ask Claude to pull them together, group by theme, and show what's relevant to your roadmap.

![Cowork for Product icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5d791f0e441cbf8d986_Check%20Circle.svg)

![Cowork for Product icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de429bc4ad0f9df61dc_Check%20Circle.svg)

### You stay the decision maker, not the bottleneck

Hand Cowork your meeting notes, docs, and emails. Get back a clean list of action items, open questions, and owners, so you can focus on the decisions that matter.

![Tool icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5ffb0b8d74135e197e8_Tool.svg)

![Tool icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726dff1439f5401deb7cfc_Tool.svg)

### You stay in control

Cowork handles the work, but you make the calls. You can choose to review outputs before they're sent, approve actions before they're taken, and stay informed every step of the way.

## Use cases for product teams

![Checklist icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5dad0a42ff9b1d9abad_Checklist.svg)

![Checklist icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de43add0651f34cb63e_Checklist.svg)

### Track commitments and follow-ups

After meetings, commitments pile up. Ask Cowork to pull your open items from Asana, Slack, and docs and get back a single list of who owes what, and what's overdue.

![Feedback icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5e0a60feb59aaf83fc6_Feedback.svg)

![Feedback icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de83add0651f34cb6eb_Feedback.svg)

### Weekly customer feedback digest

Feature requests pile up across email, Slack, and your support tool. Schedule a weekly digest and Cowork pulls them together, groups by theme, and delivers a summary tied to your roadmap every Friday.

![Check Circle icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5d791f0e441cbf8d986_Check%20Circle.svg)

![Check Circle icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de429bc4ad0f9df61dc_Check%20Circle.svg)

### Launch readiness in one check

Before launch, you need approvals, docs, and final checks. Give Claude your launch criteria and ask it to check progress across your tools so you get a clear view of what's done and what's still open.

![File icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5e037c54e08d4e2f696_File.svg)

![File icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de931b21ed6cce3f12c_File.svg)

### From problem statement to feature spec

Describe what you're building and who it's for. Cowork generates a full PRD with user stories, prioritized requirements, success metrics, and open questions.

![Chats icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5d78ec6ab6a678e6d49_Chats.svg)

![Chats icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de49b55817d85505434_Chats.svg)

### Stakeholder updates, by audience

Need a weekly exec update? A launch readiness summary for eng? Cowork pulls context from Asana, Slack, and your docs, then generates the right update for the right audience.

![Eye icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5e06a5153d1921f5d32_Eye.svg)

![Eye icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de865711b3d8cfd2d27_Eye.svg)

### Competitive briefs on demand

Pick a competitor or feature area. Cowork researches their approach, builds a feature comparison matrix, and surfaces positioning implications.
