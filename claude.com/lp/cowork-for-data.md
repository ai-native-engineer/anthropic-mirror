## Why data teams choose Cowork

Built for analysts who spend more time writing queries than finding insights.

![Cowork for Data icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5d73149d8120c3064d4_Chart.svg)

![Cowork for Data icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de40e27a9b50454f302_Chart.svg)

### From question to insight in one step

Describe what you need. Cowork writes the SQL, runs it against your warehouse, generates the chart, flags the key patterns, and synthesizes findings into actionable recommendations.

![Cowork for Data icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5c40c7210d194cbe0f6_Activity.svg)

![Cowork for Data icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726dde898e44c770f0fb12_Activity.svg)

### Dashboards and visualizations on demand

Need a dashboard for a stakeholder review? Cowork builds interactive HTML dashboards with Chart.js, dropdown filters, and clean styling from your data. No BI tool setup required.

![Cowork for Data icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5dc8ec6ab6a678e6e47_Database.svg)

![Cowork for Data icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de6740b501274d0fe7e_Database.svg)

### Works with your data warehouse

Cowork connects to Snowflake, BigQuery, Databricks, and any SQL-compatible database. It also works with CSV and Excel uploads, Google Sheets, and tools like Looker and Amplitude.

![Tool icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5ffb0b8d74135e197e8_Tool.svg)

![Tool icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726dff1439f5401deb7cfc_Tool.svg)

### You stay in control

Cowork handles the work, but you make the calls. You can choose to review outputs before they're sent, approve actions before they're taken, and stay informed every step of the way.

## Use cases for data teams

![Chart icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5d73149d8120c3064d4_Chart.svg)

![Chart icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de40e27a9b50454f302_Chart.svg)

### Ad-hoc analysis from a question

Ask "what was our monthly revenue trend by product line?" Cowork writes the SQL, executes it against your warehouse, generates a trend chart, and identifies patterns, all validated with sanity checks.

![Database icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5dc8ec6ab6a678e6e47_Database.svg)

![Database icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de6740b501274d0fe7e_Database.svg)

### Data exploration and profiling

Point Cowork at a table. Get back row counts, cardinality, null rates, unexpected values, and suggestions for high-value dimensions to analyze.

![Code icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5da60437518df485391_Code.svg)

![Code icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de68d83207682062252_Code.svg)

### Optimized SQL for any requirements

Describe a cohort retention analysis or funnel query. Cowork writes production-ready SQL for your requirements with CTEs, comments, and performance notes.

![Activity icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5c40c7210d194cbe0f6_Activity.svg)

![Activity icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726dde898e44c770f0fb12_Activity.svg)

### Interactive dashboards from your data

Upload a CSV or connect to your warehouse. Cowork builds a self-contained HTML dashboard with Chart.js visualizations, dropdown filters, and clean formatting — ready to share.

![Check Circle icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5d791f0e441cbf8d986_Check%20Circle.svg)

![Check Circle icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de429bc4ad0f9df61dc_Check%20Circle.svg)

### Pre-share validation

Before you send an analysis to stakeholders, Cowork reviews methodology, checks for bias, verifies aggregation logic, and flags issues like survivorship bias or misleading denominators.

![Calendar icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5c47fc9d77e22cedac7_Calendar.svg)

![Calendar icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de2334995cadd74cf54_Calendar.svg)

### Recurring reports without the reminder

Set up a weekly revenue summary, churn report, or pipeline snapshot once and Cowork runs it on schedule. Stakeholders get fresh data without you having to re-run the analysis every time.
