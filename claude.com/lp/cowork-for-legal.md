## Why legal teams choose Cowork

Built for teams that review more documents than they can read in a day. Cowork is available on desktop only.

![Cowork for Legal icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5dc8ec6ab6a678e6e47_Database.svg)

![Cowork for Legal icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de6740b501274d0fe7e_Database.svg)

### Query your contracts like a database

Ask Cowork "which vendors have a non-compete?" across 50+ agreements. Get back a structured comparison table with the relevant clauses pulled and organized.

![Cowork for Legal icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5d791f0e441cbf8d986_Check%20Circle.svg)

![Cowork for Legal icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de429bc4ad0f9df61dc_Check%20Circle.svg)

### Reviews run against your standards

Cowork compares incoming NDAs and contracts against your standard terms, flags every deviation, and explains why it matters, before you've opened the document yourself.

![Cowork for Legal icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5d05b8a3ca9fae51656_Batch.svg)

![Cowork for Legal icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de0861f7607e8bae01b_Batch.svg)

### Reach all your tools in one place

Cowork runs in the Claude desktop app and connects to your everyday apps, and your local files. Upload a folder of contracts or point it at a shared drive so Claude can get things done.

![Tool icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5ffb0b8d74135e197e8_Tool.svg)

![Tool icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726dff1439f5401deb7cfc_Tool.svg)

### You stay in control

Cowork handles the work, but you make the calls. You can choose to review outputs before they're sent, approve actions before they're taken, and stay informed every step of the way.

## Use cases for legal teams

![Database icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5dc8ec6ab6a678e6e47_Database.svg)

![Database icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de6740b501274d0fe7e_Database.svg)

### Contract clause querying

Point Cowork at a folder of 50+ contracts and ask a question. Get back a comparison table with the relevant provisions extracted and organized side by side.

![Edit icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5e0d9d1866577d4eb6b_Edit.svg)

![Edit icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de8d402d5c189d05f09_Edit.svg)

### NDA review and redlining

Compare an incoming NDA against your company's standard terms. Cowork flags deviations, explains the risk, and produces a redlined document with tracked changes.

![Checklist icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5dad0a42ff9b1d9abad_Checklist.svg)

![Checklist icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de43add0651f34cb63e_Checklist.svg)

### Compliance audit prep

Scan a documentation folder against a regulatory checklist on a recurring schedule. Cowork verifies which required documents exist, flags what's missing, and drafts a gap report.

![Files icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5e57fc9d77e22cee754_Files.svg)

![Files icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726dea209a6f9feca25adc_Files.svg)

### Due diligence document review

Analyze a data room of agreements, surface key risks and unusual terms, and produce a structured summary ready for review.

![Filter icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5e56d5712653b821e08_Filter.svg)

![Filter icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726dea192b0815e2241e7a_Filter.svg)

### Clause extraction across agreements

Need to find every indemnification clause, liability cap, or termination provision across your portfolio? Cowork reads them all and organizes the results.

![File icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5e037c54e08d4e2f696_File.svg)

![File icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de931b21ed6cce3f12c_File.svg)

Turn dense legal agreements into plain-language summaries for business teams, delivered via email or Slack.
