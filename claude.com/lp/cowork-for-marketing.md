## Why marketing teams choose Cowork

Built for marketing teams that spend too much time producing and not enough time thinking.

![Cowork for Marketing icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5e57fc9d77e22cee754_Files.svg)

![Cowork for Marketing icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726dea209a6f9feca25adc_Files.svg)

### One brief, campaign-ready deliverables

Hand Cowork a blog post and get back social threads, email copy, and deliverables in Powerpoint, Word, and Excel files. No need to copy and paste between tools or reformat your content.

![Cowork for Marketing icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5d73149d8120c3064d4_Chart.svg)

![Cowork for Marketing icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de40e27a9b50454f302_Chart.svg)

### Market sizing in minutes, not weeks

Need to size a market or map the competitive landscape? Cowork researches, structures the analysis, and outputs a presentation, spreadsheet, and source document with citations.

![Cowork for Marketing icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5d05b8a3ca9fae51656_Batch.svg)

![Cowork for Marketing icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726de0861f7607e8bae01b_Batch.svg)

### Works inside the tools you already use

Cowork runs in the Claude desktop app and connects to Slack, Notion, Amplitude, Figma, Asana, and more. For web workflows, Claude in Chrome goes wherever your browser takes you.

![Tool icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/6970f5ffb0b8d74135e197e8_Tool.svg)

![Tool icon](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6889473510b50328dbb70ae6/69726dff1439f5401deb7cfc_Tool.svg)

### You stay in control

Cowork handles the work, but you make the calls. You can choose to review outputs before they're sent, approve actions before they're taken, and stay informed every step of the way.

### Repurpose content across channels

Give Claude a long-form piece and get back a blog post, social threads, email copy, and a one-pager all in one session and on brand.

### From competitor question to comparison deck

Point Claude at competitor materials or a market question. Get back a structured comparison in PowerPoint with an Excel workbook.

Pull your own performance data from spreadsheets and dashboards without waiting on data science or marketing ops. Schedule it or request a one-off.

Turn rough strategy notes into a formatted campaign brief or a themed slide deck ready for review.
